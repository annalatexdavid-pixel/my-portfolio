**Building a visual communication system across digital and physical media for a Web3 software technology brand**

Turing Space is a Web3 company that leverages Decentralized Identity (DID) technology to provide digital trust solutions for governments and enterprises worldwide.

My design work focused on translating complex, abstract trust mechanisms into concrete, professional brand visuals and product experiences — bridging communication across a cross-border digital trust network.
