evoke is a personal care and bath brand founded in 2023. In this project, I took on the role of brand designer, deeply involved in the complete journey from concept to finished product.

My responsibilities spanned brand strategy and product development, visual identity system creation, packaging design, and marketing material planning — with the goal of building a uniquely integrated sensory experience for the brand, from the inside out.
