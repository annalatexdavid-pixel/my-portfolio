An immersive relaxation app that engages auditory, tactile, and visual senses, blending virtual and real-life experiences.

Placing the user in the role of a "traveler," the app creates a worldview of journeying through nature — open the app at any time, pair it with physical relaxation accessories, and drift into a series of randomized natural escapes.

## Concept

Five minutes, anytime, anywhere — step into a beautiful imagined setting. Become a carefree traveler and enjoy a relaxing journey through nature. It might be a forest adventure filled with birdsong and insects, or a refreshing trip to a cool, cascading waterfall.

Pair the app with a tactile stress-relief ball to unwind while you travel; after completing a journey, follow guided prompts to bring relaxation into your real-world self as well.
