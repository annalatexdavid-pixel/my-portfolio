**From zero to one — building a complete professional identity for the APDI international organization, from Corporate Identity System (CIS) to official website**

**This work was shortlisted for the 2025 GOOD DESIGN AWARD**

## Background

The Asia-Pacific Digital Identity (APDI) consortium is an international organization newly established in 2024, dedicated to promoting digital identity authentication cooperation across the Asia-Pacific region.

As a brand-new organization, APDI initially lacked a unified, professional public-facing image, making it difficult to effectively communicate authority and credibility on the international stage.

My challenge was to build a comprehensive brand visual system for APDI and apply it across all digital and physical touchpoints, ensuring a high degree of consistency and professionalism throughout the brand identity.

## Corporate Identity System (CIS) Development

The first step of the project was to translate the organization's core values — "connection," "professionalism," and "trust" — into a concrete visual language.

I designed a new logo, defined a professional color palette and typography system, and compiled everything into a comprehensive CIS guidelines manual to serve as the unified standard for all future design work.

> Combining Asian cultural calligraphy strokes with the letter 'A' and integrating a bridge image by connecting the strokes into a sphere, symbolizes APDI as a crucial bridge connecting international digital identities.
>
> We use gradient colors as the primary focus, symbolizing inclusivity, connect.

## Official Website | Visual Design & Development

The official website is APDI's primary digital presence. I independently handled the entire process — from information architecture and UI design to full production build using Webflow.

The design focus was on clear information communication and a seamless browsing experience, with consistent presentation across both desktop and mobile devices.
