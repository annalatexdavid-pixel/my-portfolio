JANDAN is a plant-based skincare e-commerce brand founded in 2016.

As a visual designer at JANDAN, I created campaign advertising visuals and landing pages for seasonal promotions, and led the design of product packaging and festive gift sets — elevating the brand's conversion rate and visual competitiveness in a crowded market.

In addition to maintaining brand visuals, I participated in marketing strategy planning and promotional material design across e-commerce platforms including momo, Shopee, and Pinkoi. Balancing brand identity with the visual intensity needed to drive purchases was a key challenge.
